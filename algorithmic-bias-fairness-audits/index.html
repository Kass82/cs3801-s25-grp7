What is Algorithmic Bias?

Algorithmic bias refers to the unfair outcomes that AI systems create. These outcomes usually reinforce stigmas of racial, gender, and socioeconomic biases. AI may use biased data with its biased algorithm design, lack of diversity in AI teams, or flawed training data. Meaning the data that the algorithims are used will have pre-existing prejudices that are baked into the data itself. That same data feeds back to the AI system, continuing to discriminate against people.

With fairness audits, it can help find and mitigate biases before they are used in AI systems. These audits can implement strategies to promote equal outcomes and understand how AI systems impact different groups. Organizations can ensure that AI systems are transparent and accountable by prioritizing fairness audits
